Blacksmith Coals

Blacksmith Coal Usage

Give to blacksmith to be able to infuse weapons with elements

Farron Coal (Ability to use Heavy, Sharp and Poison Gems

Sage's Coals (Ability to use Crystal, Blessed and Deep Gem)

Giant's Coal (Ability to use Lightning, Simple and Chaos Gem)

Profaned Coal (Ability to use Dark, Blood and Hollow Gem)

Drops And Locations For Blacksmith Coals

Farron Coal

At the Road of Sacrifices at the Halfway Fortress bonfire where you first meet Anri and Horace go down and drop down from the ledges on the right side keep going forward along the right wall and enter a doorway in the end go left and you will see a Black Knight guarding a couple bodies the body at the end of the hall has the coal


Sage's Coals

At the first bonfire in Farron Keep go left and you will see a structure guarded by a darkwraith the coal is in a body inside this structure


Giant's Coal

At the first bonfire in Anor Londo go up the stairs turn left into a doorway then take a right through another doorway and the coal will be on a giant's corpse


Profaned Coal

In Irythll Dungeon next to the room with the six jail cells before the shortcut
