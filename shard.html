Drops and Locations for Titanite Shards

Titanite Shard Locations

Can be purchased infinitely from the Shrine Handmaid for 800 souls each after acquiring the Dreamchaser's Ashes or the Grave Warden's Ashes.
Dropped by Winged Knights in the High Wall of Lothric and Lothric Castle.
Dropped by Evangelists in the Undead Settlement
Dropped by Hollow Manservants (the enemies with large machetes and cages on their backs) in the Undead Settlement.
Dropped by most Hollows in the High Wall of Lothric, Undead Settlement, and Road of Sacrifices.
Dropped by the Silver Knights in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley (x2 guaranteed from the archer in the painting room).
Dropped by the Silver Knights in Anor Londo (almost guaranteed drop).
Dropped from various enemies in the Smouldering Lake area (common).
Dropped by Red-Eyed enemies in the Catacombs of Carthus.
